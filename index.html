About Us

We are Care Compass Hospitals, and we provide

Care Compass Hospitals is a leading private hospital network registered under the Ministry of Health (MoH). With branches in Kandy, Colombo, and Kurunegala, we are committed to delivering exceptional healthcare services. Our facilities include advanced laboratories, a wide range of medical treatments, and online services such as appointment scheduling, medical record access, and bill payments. Our team of experienced doctors and staff are dedicated to providing personalized care to every patient.

Our Services

Medical Treatments

We offer a wide range of medical treatments, including general medicine, surgery, pediatrics, cardiology, and more. Our expert doctors ensure personalized care for every patient.


Specialty Services

Our specialty services include oncology, neurology, orthopedics, and maternity care. We use advanced technology to provide the best treatment options.


Lab & Medical Tests

Our state-of-the-art laboratory offers a comprehensive range of tests, including blood tests, imaging, and pathology services, with accurate and timely results.


Our Facilities

State-of-the-Art Facilities & Services

At Care Compass Hospitals, we pride ourselves on offering world-class facilities to ensure the best care for our patients. Our hospitals are equipped with advanced medical technology, modern operation theaters, and comfortable patient rooms. We also provide specialized services such as emergency care, intensive care units (ICUs), and maternity wards. Our goal is to create a healing environment that prioritizes patient comfort and safety.
